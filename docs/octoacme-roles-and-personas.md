# OctoAcme Personas

This document defines typical roles and responsibilities used in OctoAcme project docs and exercises.

---

## Developers

### Role Summary
Developers design, build, test, and deliver software components. They collaborate with product and project leads to implement features that meet acceptance criteria and quality standards.

### Responsibilities
- Implement features and fixes to meet acceptance criteria
- Write and maintain tests and documentation
- Participate in design and code reviews
- Assist in estimating and planning work
- Help identify technical risks and propose mitigations

### Goals
- Deliver reliable, maintainable code
- Reduce cycle time from idea to production
- Maintain high test coverage and observability

### Typical Communication
- Daily standups and sprint planning
- PR descriptions and code review comments
- Technical design docs when needed

---

## Product Managers

### Role Summary
Product Managers define what should be built to deliver customer and business value. They own the product vision, prioritize the backlog, and measure outcomes.

### Responsibilities
- Define problem statements and success metrics
- Prioritize the roadmap and backlog
- Collaborate with stakeholders and engineering on trade-offs
- Validate solutions through user research and metrics

### Goals
- Maximize customer value and impact
- Make clear, data-driven prioritization decisions
- Ensure product-market fit and usability

### Typical Communication
- Weekly alignment with PM and engineering leads
- Roadmap updates and stakeholder briefings
- Acceptance criteria and feature specs

---

## Project Managers

### Role Summary
Project Managers coordinate delivery activities, manage schedules, risks, and communications. They enable the team to deliver on commitments efficiently.

### Responsibilities
- Create and maintain project plans and timelines
- Manage risks, dependencies, and resource constraints
- Facilitate meetings (kickoff, planning, retrospectives)
- Ensure consistent project documentation and status reporting
- Coordinate cross-team and stakeholder communication

### Goals
- Deliver projects on time and within scope
- Minimize unplanned work and escalations
- Maintain transparency and alignment across stakeholders

### Typical Communication
- Weekly status updates and stakeholder reports
- Risk registers and decision logs
- Coordination via project boards and meeting facilitation

---

## QA/Testing Lead

### Role Summary
QA/Testing Leads ensure product quality through comprehensive testing strategies, test execution, and quality validation. They collaborate with developers and product managers to define acceptance criteria and testing approaches.

### Responsibilities
- Define and maintain quality standards and test plans
- Execute unit, integration, and end-to-end testing
- Validate acceptance criteria before feature sign-off
- Document test results and quality metrics
- Identify and triage defects with severity levels
- Coordinate security and performance testing
- Approve features for release

### Goals
- Ensure high product quality and user satisfaction
- Reduce production defects and support burden
- Maintain comprehensive test coverage

### Typical Communication
- Weekly quality sync with PM and engineering
- Test results in sprint reviews
- Defect reports and severity assessments

---

## Stakeholder/Sponsor

### Role Summary
Stakeholders and Sponsors provide business context, approve budgets/resources, and ensure projects align with organizational priorities. They make go/no-go decisions and provide executive visibility.

### Responsibilities
- Approve project initiation and resource allocation
- Provide business context and success criteria
- Make key trade-off and prioritization decisions
- Receive monthly stakeholder updates and reviews
- Escalate blockers at executive level if needed
- Sign off on major releases or milestones

### Goals
- Ensure projects deliver business value
- Maintain alignment with organizational strategy
- Reduce delivery risk and uncertainty

### Typical Communication
- Monthly stakeholder updates
- Milestone reviews and demos
- Escalation notifications for critical issues

---

## Product Lead

### Role Summary
Product Leads provide strategic product direction and oversight. They review and approve product decisions, align roadmaps across teams, and ensure consistency with product vision.

### Responsibilities
- Review and approve project charters and success metrics
- Align product decisions across multiple teams or projects
- Ensure feature alignment with product vision and roadmap
- Participate in escalation of product trade-offs
- Review release communications and customer messaging
- Mentor Product Managers on strategy and prioritization

### Goals
- Maintain coherent product strategy and vision
- Ensure cross-team alignment and consistency
- Maximize overall product value and market impact

### Typical Communication
- Weekly syncs with Product Managers
- Quarterly strategy and roadmap reviews
- Ad-hoc escalations for major decisions

---

## Security Engineer

### Role Summary
Security Engineers integrate security into product delivery. They conduct threat reviews, implement security controls, manage incidents, and ensure compliance.

### Responsibilities
- Participate in threat modeling for new features
- Review security requirements and acceptance criteria
- Conduct security testing and vulnerability scanning
- Respond to security incidents following incident playbook
- Maintain security documentation and known issue registry
- Advise on secure development practices

### Goals
- Deliver secure, compliant products
- Minimize security vulnerabilities and incidents
- Build security awareness across the organization

### Typical Communication
- Design reviews for security-sensitive features
- Security incident notifications and response
- Quarterly security training and awareness

---

## Team Lead / Scrum Master

### Role Summary
Team Leads and Scrum Masters facilitate team coordination, remove blockers, and ensure adherence to team processes. They serve the team by enabling smooth execution.

### Responsibilities
- Facilitate daily standups and sprint ceremonies
- Track and communicate team capacity and velocity
- Identify and escalate blockers and dependencies
- Maintain sprint board and backlog health
- Coach team on process compliance and continuous improvement
- Support retrospectives and action item tracking

### Goals
- Enable team autonomy and self-organization
- Maintain predictable delivery velocity
- Reduce process friction and bottlenecks

### Typical Communication
- Daily standups and sprint planning
- Weekly capacity and progress reviews
- Retrospective facilitation and action tracking
